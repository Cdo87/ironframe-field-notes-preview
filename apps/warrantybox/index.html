Listing copy

Safe listing copy draft

First paragraph

Warrantrail helps you keep purchase proof organized before you need it. Save a receipt photo, purchase date, retailer, return deadline, warranty expiry, serial number, and notes for each item — then get local reminders before important dates arrive.

What you can track

  • Product name, retailer, purchase date, and category
  • Receipt photo and notes
  • Return-window deadline and warranty-expiry date
  • Local reminder settings
  • Export/delete controls for your own records

Safety boundary

  • No refund guarantees or savings claims
  • No legal, warranty-eligibility, or claim advice
  • No claim submission, recall monitoring, or merchant integrations
  • No cloud account required for the MVP direction

Monetization hypothesis

Freemium now, lifetime unlock candidate later

Free proof tier

  • Up to 5 saved items
  • Receipt photo per item
  • One local reminder per item
  • Delete/export single-item data

Paid unlock hypothesis

  • Unlimited saved items
  • Multiple reminders per item
  • CSV export
  • Future user-controlled backup bundle

Revenue quality gate

Prove repeat use before paid complexity

Before any StoreKit, subscription, App Store, or public launch action, Warrantrail needs local evidence that the habit is worth keeping: users can add proof quickly, return to upcoming deadlines, export their records, and understand that the paid unlock is organization depth rather than refund success.

Do not monetize a one-time gimmick

  • Track local retention proxies first: items saved, reminders scheduled, exports created, and return visits to soonest-deadline views.
  • Keep the first paid hypothesis as lifetime unlock until repeated use justifies recurring revenue.
  • Do not use revenue, retention, buyer, or app-store claims until those numbers exist from approved tests.
  • Kill or defer paid launch if the prototype cannot beat Notes plus Reminders without risky automation, cloud sync, or warranty/refund promises.
