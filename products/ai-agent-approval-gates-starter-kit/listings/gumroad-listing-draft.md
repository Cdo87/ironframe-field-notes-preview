## Product title

AI Agent Approval Gates Starter Kit

## Subtitle

A practical starter kit for founders using AI agents without letting them silently spend, publish, message, expose secrets, delete, or trade.

## Short description

AI agents are useful when they can work locally and stop cleanly before external risk. This starter kit gives you a simple founder control system: what agents can do alone, what requires approval, what is prohibited by default, and what evidence must be left before a task is considered complete.

## Who it is for

- Solo founders experimenting with AI agents.
- Operators using Claude Code, Codex, Hermes Agent, or automation workflows.
- Small teams that want AI help without silent external risk.
- Creators building content/product systems with agents.

## What is included

- AI Agent Approval Gate Checklist.
- Founder AI Autonomy Policy.
- Agent Task Card Template.
- Risk Register Template.
- Approval sentence examples.
- Evidence-before-completion checklist.

## What it helps prevent

- Silent publishing.
- Silent spend or paid-credit use.
- Secret/API-key exposure.
- Unapproved outreach.
- Deletion or irreversible changes.
- Production deploys without evidence.
- Real-money trading/betting by agents.
