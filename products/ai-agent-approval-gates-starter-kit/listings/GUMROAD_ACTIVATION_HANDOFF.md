### Short description

AI agents are useful when they can work locally and stop cleanly before external risk. This starter kit gives you a simple founder control system: what agents can do alone, what requires approval, what is prohibited by default, and what evidence must be left before a task is considered complete.

### Full description

AI agents are becoming useful enough to delegate research, drafting, coding, content preparation, and operations work. The risk is usually not malicious intent. The risk is vague permission.

This Starter Kit gives founders and operators a simple approval-gate system before agents touch publishing, money, customer messages, secrets, deletion, production systems, or trading.

Inside the pack:

- AI Agent Approval Gate Checklist.
- Founder AI Autonomy Policy.
- Agent Task Card Template.
- Risk Register Template.
- Agent Permission Matrix.
- First 7 Days of Agent Delegation mini-playbook.
- Printable PDF checklist.
- Markdown/editable versions.
- Start-here guide, changelog, and licence/use notes.

Use it to define:

- green actions an agent may usually perform locally;
- amber actions requiring explicit approval;
- red actions prohibited by default;
- evidence required before a task is marked complete;
- rollback and review expectations for delegated work.

This product provides operational templates only. It is not legal, financial, tax, cybersecurity, compliance, investment, employment, or trading advice. You remain responsible for how you configure, supervise, and authorise AI tools.
